Overview

An animated short film that weaves a dynamic journey through the perspectives of different life forms in a futuristic city.

Storyline

Below is the general storyline:

  • Water Perspective: The story begins with the perspective of a fish swimming underwater. The audience can observe the underwater scenery, potentially featuring remnants of human civilization (such as old buildings or tools).
  • Sky Perspective: The fish is caught by a bird, and the camera transitions to the birdâ€™s perspective. From high above, the cyberpunk city comes into view, with neon lights illuminating the night sky, alongside visible transportation networks and high-tech buildings.
  • Ground Perspective: The bird becomes prey to a stray cat, and the perspective shifts to the catâ€™s point of view. The cat navigates the city streets and pipes, highlighting the darker, more chaotic side of the urban environment.
  • Chase Perspective: A dog spots the cat and begins a pursuit, shifting the perspective to the dogâ€™s first-person view. This sequence takes the audience through a high-energy chase across various corners of the city.
  • Human Perspective: Finally, the dog is reunited with its owner, transitioning to the human perspective. The human walks the dog back home and opens a book, where the short filmâ€™s title, inspirations, and creator credits are displayed on the pages.
